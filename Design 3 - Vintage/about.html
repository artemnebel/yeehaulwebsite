★ A letter from our founder ★

Started in one truck. Same code, every job.

Yeehaul started in 2019 when I helped a neighbor clear out an old garage and realized just how hard it was to find an honest, friendly junk removal crew. Big chains were expensive and impersonal. Cheap guys disappeared after the deposit.

So I built the company I wished existed. One truck, one promise:

"Show up on time. Charge what we quoted. Leave the place better than we found it."

Five years and thousands of jobs later, that promise hasn't changed. Our crews keep neighborhoods clear, partner with local charities to donate every reusable item, and recycle more than 80% of what we collect.

Junk removal might sound like a small thing, but when your garage finally has space again — or a loved one's estate is handled with care — it isn't small at all.

Jake T.

Founder · Lead Hauler
