About us

Hauling done honest. Howdy, neighbor.

We're a locally owned crew on a simple mission: take the headache out of clearing your space. Fair prices, on-time arrivals, and treating your home like it was our own.

Our story

Started in a single truck. Still treats every job the same.

Yeehaul started in 2026 when our founder helped a neighbor clear out an old garage and realized just how hard it was to find an honest, friendly junk removal crew. The big chains were expensive and impersonal; the cheap guys disappeared after taking a deposit.

So we built the company we wished existed. One truck, one promise: show up on time, charge what we quoted, and leave the place better than we found it. Since then, with thousands of jobs under our belts, that promise hasn't changed.

Today our crews keep neighborhoods clear, donate every reusable item we come across, and recycle more than 80% of what we collect. Junk removal might sound like a small thing, but when your garage finally has space again or your loved one's estate is handled with care — it isn't small at all.
