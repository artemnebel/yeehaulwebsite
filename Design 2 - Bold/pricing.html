/ FAQ

Got questions?

Why is my final price different than the online estimate?

+

Online estimates are based on averages. Your on-site quote reflects actual truck space, weight, and item type. You always see the price before any work starts.

Do you charge extra for stairs?

+

Standard residential stairs and reasonable walking distances are included. Extreme situations (4+ flights, 100+ ft walks) may carry a modest surcharge — always disclosed in your on-site quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

All major credit & debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. We process payment after the work is done.

Senior, military, or repeat-customer discounts?

+

Yes — 10% off for seniors, military, and first responders. Repeat customers get a $25 credit on every third booking. Just mention it when you book.

Is there a minimum charge?

+

Yes — minimum is $129 to cover the truck, fuel, and crew time. That covers a single small piece up to about 1/8 of a truckload.
