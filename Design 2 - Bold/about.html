/ The story

Started in one truck. Same code, every job.

Yeehaul started in 2019 when our founder helped a neighbor clear out an old garage and realized just how hard it was to find an honest, friendly junk removal crew. Big chains were expensive and impersonal. Cheap guys disappeared after the deposit.

Show up on time. Charge what we quoted. Leave the place better than we found it.

Five years and thousands of jobs later, that promise hasn't changed. Our crews keep neighborhoods clear, partner with local charities to donate everything reusable, and recycle more than 80% of what we collect.
